About Me

Hi! My name is Rahul Brahmal and I am a sophomore Computer Science Major at Georgia Institute of Technology, with a concentration in Information Internetworks and Intelligence. During my freshmen year and half of my sophomore year, I was a Mechanical Engineering Major.

While I was born in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, I have had the opportunity to live in various different cities during my formative education. These cities include Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, Singapore, Singapore, Palo Alto, California, New Delhi, India and now, Atlanta, Georgia. The opportunity to live all over the world, coupled with my experience in adapting to very different environments gives me the perspective to understand the varying needs of users in international markets.

I also have a few hobbies, which include geeking out over new technology, investing in stocks and FOREX markets, obsessing over fitness and eating healthy, and keeping up to date on the latest in politics.
